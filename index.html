Privacy Policy

Last Updated: 15/3/2025

Welcome to Rafiqy’s Privacy Policy. We are committed to protecting your privacy and ensuring that your personal data is handled in a safe and responsible manner. This Privacy Policy outlines the types of information we collect, how we use it, whom we share it with, and the measures we take to keep it secure.

By using our mobile application (the “Service”), you consent to the collection and use of your data as described in this Privacy Policy. If you do not agree with any part of this policy, please discontinue using our Service immediately.

1. Information We Collect

1.1 Feedback Data

Users can voluntarily submit feedback through our app to help us improve our services. This feedback may include suggestions, bug reports, and general comments. We do not request personally identifiable information in feedback submissions. However, if a user voluntarily includes such details in their feedback, we will handle that information with strict confidentiality.

1.2 Crash and Error Reports

We collect crash reports and application not responding (ANR) logs through Google Play Services to diagnose and fix issues within the app. The collected data may include:

  • Device model and manufacturer
  • Operating system version
  • App version at the time of the crash
  • System logs related to the issue

This data helps us identify and resolve performance problems but does not contain personally identifiable information.

2. How We Use Your Data

  • Enhance the performance and stability of our application
  • Investigate and fix reported issues
  • Improve the user experience based on feedback
  • Monitor app usage trends and diagnose technical problems
  • Ensure compliance with legal and regulatory requirements

We do not use the collected data for marketing, advertising, or tracking purposes.

3. Data Sharing and Disclosure

We do not use third-party services to collect, process, or analyze user data. Our application operates independently without integrating external analytics tools, advertising networks, or payment processors.

3.2 Legal Compliance

We may disclose information if required by law, regulation, or legal process, including responding to court orders or government requests.

4. Data Storage and Security

We take reasonable precautions to protect your data against unauthorized access, alteration, disclosure, or destruction. These measures include encryption, access control, and security audits. However, no security system is completely foolproof, and we cannot guarantee absolute security.

5. Data Retention

We retain collected data only for as long as necessary to fulfill the purposes outlined in this policy. Specifically:

  • Feedback data is stored for analysis and deleted when no longer needed.
  • Crash reports are retained for troubleshooting and historical tracking of issues.

Once the data is no longer needed, we ensure its secure deletion.
